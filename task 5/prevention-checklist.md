# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

## General Safety Tips
- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts (avoid using the same password everywhere)
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps
- Do not click on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp
- Always verify unknown contacts before sharing personal information
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts
- Regularly check your bank statements and online activity for any unusual transactions
- Limit the amount of personal information you share on social media

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — it is only required for sending money
- Do not approve unknown "Collect Requests" in apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm
- Avoid scanning random QR codes sent by strangers
- Always double-check the receiver’s name before making any payment

## Reporting Cybercrime in India
- Report online fraud at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (immediate assistance for financial fraud)

## Final Reminder
Stay alert, think before you click, and never trust unknown online requests blindly. Awareness is your first line of defense against cybercrime.
